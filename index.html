The Beginning

MMEMSA started with a small group of visionary students in Medicare,Mengo & then ECUREI.

The Medicare Mengo ECUREI Muslim Students Association (MMEMSA) was established to create a sanctuary for Muslim students across the major health institutions in Mengo. We believe that a healthcare worker's skills are doubled when driven by a compassionate, God-fearing heart.

Institutions: Medicare, ECUREI, Mengo Training School

Our history is defined by the collaborative spirit of students who refused to let institutional boundaries separate the Ummah.

Vision

To produce the most competent and ethically upright Muslim healthcare professionals in East Africa.

Mission

Empowering members through academic support, spiritual guidance, and impactful community service.
